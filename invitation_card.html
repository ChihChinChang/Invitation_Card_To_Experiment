Aims研究目的

This study is part of a Master's dissertation at the University of Nottingham's School of English. It aims to inform and improve educational instruction for Taiwan Mandarin-English bilinguals, helping them acquire useful English phrases and enhance overall English fluency.

本研究為諾丁漢大學英語學院碩士論文之一部分，旨在了解並改善台灣中英雙語者的英語教學方式，協助其習得實用的英語片語並提升整體英語流暢度。

📖
What You Will Do研究內容

You'll read short sentences on screen at your own pace to help you learn phrases like tea and toast, presented in three short parts. Afterwards, a quick questionnaire wraps things up. Your accuracy is only used to help educators improve teaching — it's not an intelligence test.

您將以自己的步調在螢幕上閱讀短句，藉此學習像 tea and toast 這樣的片語，共分三個短篇進行。結束後會有一份簡短問卷。您的正確率僅用於協助教育者改善教學方法，並非智力測驗。
